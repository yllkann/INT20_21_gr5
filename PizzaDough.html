Pizza Dough


Ingredients






Preparation

  • Yield: 8 servings
  • Prep Time: 45 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 55 minutes
  • Course: Bread
  • Cuisine: Italian
  • Author: Sabrina Snyder
Instructions
  1. In a stand mixer mix the water, sugar and yeast together and let it sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Add in the flour, oil, salt and garlic powder and whisk together on medium speed until well combined, then swap out for the dough hook and let it knead for 5 minutes on medium speed.
  3. Remove the dough hook and let it rest, covered with a damp clean towel for 30 minutes before using.
  4. Roll the pizza dough out with a bit of flour and when ready to use bake at 425 degrees topped with your favorite ingredients for 10-12 minutes.
