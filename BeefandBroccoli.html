Beef and Broccoli


Ingredients






Preparation

  • Yield: 5 servings
  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 40 minutes
  • Course: Main Dish
  • Cuisine: Chinese
Instructions
  1. Add everything except the canola oil, garlic, ginger and broccoli into a large bowl and mix well, and let marinate for at least 15 minutes.
  2. In a wok add canola oil on high heat and add in half the beef and cook for 1-2 minutes on each side before cooking the second half of the beef another 1-2 minutes on each side.
  3. Add in the garlic and ginger, stir and cook for 30 seconds and pour in the marinade and broccoli, cover and cook until the sauce thickens before adding the beef back in to coat with sauce.
