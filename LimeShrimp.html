Preparation

  • Cook Time: 10 hours
  • Total Time: 30 minutes
  • Course: Dinner
  • Cuisine: American
  • Author: Sabrina Snyder
Instructions
  1. Mix cilantro, lime zest, lime juice, olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Toss the shrimp with the marinade and let marinate for 15 minutes.
  3. Heat your grill or grill pan to medium heat. If using a grill, skewer the shrimp on metal or soaked wooden skewers.
  4. Grill the shrimp, 2-3 minutes per side, until the shrimp is bright pink.
